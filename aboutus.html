About Us

Creating connections between humans and fur babies

Our Mission

We further our mission by placing our fur babies into nuturing homes by working with both cat and dog rescues and providing resources to organizations that have the same dedication as us in improving the lives of cats and dogs.

FAQ's

Do I need to book a visit to the cafe lounge ahead of time?

Walk-in spots are available for each session, but space is limited, reservations ahead of time is strongly encouraged.

Is there an age restriction for the cat lounge?

We currently welcome all visitors 18 years old and older. Children 6 years old and older are welcome with the accompany of an adult.

Where do the fur babies come from?

We foster our fur babies from multiple animal shelters near us.

Are the fur babies sprayed/neutered and vaccinated?

Yes, all our fur babies have been sprayed or neutered and vaccinated at the shelter before coming to the Fur Babies Cafe.
